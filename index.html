35+ Years of Excellence

Since our modest beginning in 1989, Panchali Furniture & Interiors has been synonymous with quality, comfort, and exceptional craftsmanship. We are one of the oldest and most reputed furniture showrooms in Navi Mumbai.

Our obsessive pursuit for quality has paid us rich dividends. We constantly modify and reinvent our products as per ever-changing needs, ensuring our privileged customers have a happy and comfortable experience.

We specialize in manufacturing attractive, ergonomically well-designed furniture coupled with utmost comfort, ease of handling, and maintenance. Every piece bearing our name is sturdy and chiseled to perfection.
